About Build to Wear

Build to Wear is a platform dedicated to supporting and empowering fashion entrepreneurs who are building sustainable, innovative, and meaningful brands.

Our mission is to help founders navigate the complex world of fashion entrepreneurship by providing resources, connections, and opportunities to grow their businesses.

We believe in the power of sustainable fashion and its ability to create positive change in the industry. Through our platform, we connect founders with mentors, investors, and like-minded entrepreneurs who share their vision.

Our Values

Sustainability

We believe in creating fashion that respects both people and the planet.

Innovation

We support founders who are pushing boundaries and redefining what's possible in fashion.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where founders can learn, grow, and succeed together.
